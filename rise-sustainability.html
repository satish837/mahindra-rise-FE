Greening Ourselves

We are on an accelerated path to a sustainable, low-carbon business to reduce our environmental footprint across operations

Scope 1+2 emission reduction
  • Scope 1+2 emission reduction
  • Adopting Material Circularity
  • Becoming Water Positive

M&M adopted a 60-MWp captive solar plant at Parbhani, Maharashtra to achieve 50% renewable electricity by 2025.

Energy productivity of auto sector has increased by 55% and farm sector by 61% compared to 2009.

MHRIL Kanha: India’s 1st 100% Solar Powered Resort.

Mahindra Heavy Engines Ltd. 1st Indian Company to double its energy productivity.

71% of waste generated by M&M was recycled & reused.

Mahindra Eden designed for 90% waste recyclability.

Mahindra Group has been “water positive” since 2014.

Decarbonizing Our Industries

We are enabling our supply chain to decarbonize themselves, and pushing the industry’s shift to the use of electric vehicles & alternate fuels to shape the future of mobility / accelerate the transition to clean mobility.

  • Supporting Net-Zero Supply Chain
  • Accelerating EV Transition
  • Auto-recycling Adoption

Supplier Code of Conduct for all M&M suppliers

Nearly 600 suppliers, dealers and distributors trained in & assessed on sustainability.

Sustainability programs (LED lights, motor efficiency, etc.) and training with select suppliers

Largest electric 3-wheeler company in the country with >70% market share.

Mahindra is the 1st Indian manufacturer to surpass 400 million electric kilometres.

Mahindra CERO: India’s 1st Automotive & Steel recycling facility present in 22 Indian cities.
